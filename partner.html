About Our Partnerships

Addae - Global Services is not a shipping company. We operate as a referral and affiliate business that connects clients with trusted third-party shipping and logistics companies. We also promote partner businesses and services across our websites and digital platforms.

Our goal is to help shipping companies, logistics providers, and related businesses reach more customers while ensuring clients are directed to reliable service providers.

Who Can Partner With Us

  • Shipping and logistics companies
  • Freight forwarders and courier services
  • Customs clearing and export agents
  • E-commerce fulfillment providers
  • Businesses seeking online promotion and referrals

Partnership Benefits

  • Increased visibility through our websites and online channels
  • Qualified customer referrals without upfront advertising costs
  • Affiliate or referral-based commission structure
  • Brand exposure to international clients
  • Long-term promotional and referral opportunities
